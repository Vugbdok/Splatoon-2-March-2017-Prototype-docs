# Debug Controls & Commands Used in Splatoon 2 (March Prototype)

Since this is a prototype game created four months before the official release, there are several debug commands and controls that the developers used while creating Splatoon 2.

# Dev Menus
These are all of the commands found within the game's developer menus.

## Scene Select Menu

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Some scenes can cause the game to completely crash or freeze. Opening this menu on the boot screen will also cause a crash/freeze.

While in the plaza or anywhere else in the game (other than the boot screen), you can press and hold <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_L.png" alt="Button L" width="25"  /> + <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_DPadUP.png" alt="Dpad up" width="18" /> for around 2 to 3 seconds to bring up the scene select menu shown here:

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/SSM.jpg" alt="The Dev Menu opened showing a map selected." height="365" />

### Start a Scene
Some scenes allow you to press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> to start them. However, other scenes do not support this when you encounter this issue, highlight the scene you want and press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_+.png" alt="Button +" width="18"  /> to launch it instead. You can also press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_X.png" alt="Button X" width="18"  /> to select a specific mode, such as Recon, for that scene.

# In-Game Commands
These are all of the commands that can be used directly in-game while you are outside of the Dev menus.

## Map debug
To soft reset a map press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_L.png" alt="Button L" width="25"  /> + <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_DPadDOWN.png" alt="Dpad down" width="18"  />. A soft reset reloads the map but keeps you in the same spot.

To hard reset a map press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_L.png" alt="Button L" width="25"  /> + <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_DPadUP.png" alt="Dpad up" width="18"  />. A hard reset reloads the map.

To reset the ink press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_ZL.png" alt="Button ZL" width="25"  /> + <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_DPadRIGHT.png" alt="Dpad right" width="18"  />. This will reset all ink in map.

## Debug Moving
To activate Debug Moving (flying), press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> and use the <img src="/assets/Bin/STICK_R.png" alt="Right stick" width="20"  /> stick to fly up and down.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/DBM.jpg" alt="The Debug moving being shown." height="365" />

While in Debug Moving, you can pass through walls and by holding <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_ZL.png" alt="Button ZL" width="25"  /> to transform into a squid you can move much faster.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/DBMsquid.jpg" alt="The Debug moving being shown." height="365" />

## Switching Players
During a battle, you can switch players by holding <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> and tilting the <img src="/assets/Bin/STICK_R.png" alt="Right stick" width="20"  /> stick left or right.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/STp1.jpg" alt="Team switching shown on team 1." height="365" />

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/STp2.jpg" alt="Team switching shown on team 2." height="365" />

## Switching Abiliities
You can switch abiliities by holding <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> and tilting the <img src="/assets/Bin/STICK_R.png" alt="Right stick" width="20"  /> stick down.

## Match debug
To use Match debug hold down <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> and tilt the <img src="/assets/Bin/STICK_R.png" alt="Right stick" width="20"  /> stick up.

---

> [!NOTE]
> If you have Ultrahand installed, continuously holding the <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> button will disable its API notifications.

## GamePad Recorder
The GamePad Recorder allows you to record your controller inputs and play them back automatically.

Press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_-.png" alt="Button -" width="18"  /> + <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> to open this menu:

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPR.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened." height="365" />

### Using the GamePad Recorder

Hold <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> and use the <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_DPad.png" alt="Dpad" width="18"  /> to navigate the menu options.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRDel.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on del." height="365" />

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRRec.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on rec." height="365" />

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRPlay.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on play." height="365" />

> [!TIP]
> If an option text is white, it is currently not selectable.
> 
> <img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPR.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened." height="365" />

To begin recording, press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> on the "Rec" label. The "Rec" label will begin flashing red to indicate that it is recording.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRRec.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on rec." height="365" />

Press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> again to stop.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRPlay.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on play." height="365" />

Here you can press <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> on the "Play" label to playback your recording.

You can delete your current recording by highlighting the "Del" label and pressing the <img src="/assets/Bin/BIN_A.png" alt="Button A" width="18"  /> button.

<img src="/assets/screenshots/Control/GPRDel.jpg" alt="GamePad Recorder menu opened on del." height="365" />
